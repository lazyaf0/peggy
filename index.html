Peggy is the social marketplace that allows you to discover, buy, and sell art. A place for the art-curious and the art-expert, for the art gallery and artist. A new way forward for the art world.

Let us do the vetting

Peggy has partnered with 30 contemporary art galleries from around the world to bring you the most sought-after art on the market. Featuring artists that push the boundaries of contemporary art. All artwork is original and made by highly acclaimed artists.

Authenticated by technology

Peggy’s patent-pending digital fingerprinting ensures the art you receive is the art you bought. Simply use your phone’s camera to confirm the authenticity of any purchase and register ownership. This gives you the agency to buy and sell art on the secondary market—easily and securely.
